Frequently Asked Questions

       
         
           

Is SolarMist safe for children?

           

Yes, safe for all ages and skin types.

         
         
           

Will it mess up hair or makeup?

           

We use a lightweight dry mist with a soft finish.

         
         
           

What if I have sensitive skin?

           

Our formulas are dermatologically tested and fragrance-free.

         
         
           

Is it hygienic between users?

           

Yes, the booth is fully self-cleaning and contact-free.

         
         
           

Can I buy one for home use?

           

We're starting with public-use locations, home units may follow.

         
         
           

How long does the protection last?

           

Similar to traditional sunscreen: up to 2 hours, reapply after swimming.

         
         
           

Where will booths be located?

           

Beaches, waterparks, resorts and public outdoor spaces across the UK.

         
         
           

Can I invest or collaborate?

           

We're open to investor and pilot discussions. Use the contact form below.
